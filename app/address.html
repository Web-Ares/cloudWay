and the Skype Operations Framework (SOF)

We believe that a successful project is no coincidence, it depends on a successful, well thought of methodology. At CloudWay we use the Skype Operations Framework (SOF) as the core for our approach to Skype for Business Online implementations.

The SOF provides a standardized method and approach on how to collect the right information, analyze it and build a Skype for Business Online design that best meets the needs of the customer.

The SOF is an all-around approach that also provides the guidelines on how to go through adoption and system operations to maintain and support it in the simplest way possible.

Neway Technologies is a Certified SOF Partner. Combining SOF In all of our projects is essential for successful implementation. We use SOF to plan, integrate and maximize Cloudway’s full potential, quickly and efficiently.

Being a SOF qualified partner, Neway Technologies can provide not only end to end support to the customers, but also the most value out of their investment.

SOF is designed as a repetitive cycle, always striving for improvement, and provides the foundation for customers and partners to accelerate their Skype for Business Online journey.

This is a major benefit for many who in the past have had to work out how to navigate the deployment journey on their own, investing significant time and effort to develop their own delivery methodology from the ground up.

Customers and Partners can now build their own delivery capabilities using the SOF delivery methodology as the foundation.
